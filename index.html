Our Story

Arauca Floor Sanding & Polishing is a dedicated timber flooring specialist based in Queensland. For over 15 years, we have served the Gold Coast, Brisbane, and surrounding communities with expert floor sanding and polishing services tailored to both residential and small commercial properties.

With meticulous attention to detail and a passion for timber restoration, Arauca reinvigorates floors with renewed shine and durability — bringing everyday spaces back to life, one plank at a time.

Our Services

Premium restoration services to protect and elevate your property.

Floor Sanding

We strip away years of wear, tear, and old coatings to reveal the natural, raw beauty of your timber. Our dust-minimizing equipment ensures a smooth, flawless foundation ready for coating.

Floor Polishing

Protect and enhance your timber with our high-grade polishing finishes. From matte to high-gloss, our protective coatings offer exceptional durability and a stunning, long-lasting shine.
